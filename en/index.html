Frequently Asked Questions

What you want to know

What is a carbon credit? +
A carbon credit is a tradable certificate representing the reduction or offset of 1 ton of CO₂ emissions.
How do I get a certificate? +
After calculating your carbon footprint, you can choose a suitable package, make payment, and receive your digital certificate instantly.
What payment methods are accepted? +
You can pay with credit card, bank transfer, and cryptocurrency.
How do I integrate the API? +
You can quickly integrate by reviewing our API documentation. Our enterprise support team is ready to help.
